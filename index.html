Privacy Policy of FF Family & Friends Auto Repair

Last updated: April 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the information of users of the FF Family & Friends Auto Repair app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and other details provided during registration.
  • Appointment Data: Information about scheduled services, such as date, time, and type of service.
  • Images: Photos related to the progress of the service, provided exclusively by the repair shop.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as interactions and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Manage service appointments.

  • Send important notifications related to services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When necessary to perform the contracted services.

4. Storage and Security

We take technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Request the cessation of the use of your data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing, where applicable.

To exercise your rights, please contact us through the channels below.
